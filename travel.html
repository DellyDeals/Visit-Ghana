Travel Tips for Visiting Ghana

Helpful information to make your trip safe, enjoyable, and memorable

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Ghana is during the dry season, which runs from November to March. During this period, the weather is generally warm and suitable for outdoor activities and sightseeing.

Safety Tips

Ghana is known for its hospitality and safety. Visitors are advised to remain aware of their surroundings, avoid displaying valuables openly, and follow local guidelines to ensure a safe experience.

Transportation

Transportation in Ghana includes taxis, ride-hailing services, buses, and domestic flights. In major cities, taxis and ride-hailing apps are commonly used for convenience.

Accommodation

Ghana offers a wide range of accommodation options, including hotels, guesthouses, and lodges. Visitors are encouraged to book accommodation in advance, especially during peak travel seasons.

Currency and Payments

The official currency of Ghana is the Ghana Cedi (GHS). Mobile money is widely used, and cash is commonly accepted in local markets, while cards are accepted in many hotels and restaurants.

Language

English is the official language of Ghana, making communication easy for international visitors. Local languages such as Twi, Ga, and Ewe are also widely spoken.
